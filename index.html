Game Description

Bloons Card Storm is a unique hybrid of tower defense and collectible card games. Strategically deploy powerful units, upgrade your deck, and defend against waves of enemy bloons. Choose your heroes wisely, unlock devastating spells, and compete in PvP battles to prove your strategic prowess. With regular updates, events, and an ever-growing card collection, this game guarantees endless hours of fun!

FAQ

  • What is Bloons Card Storm?
    It’s a strategy-based card game with tower defense elements where you build your deck, deploy powerful units, and defend against enemy bloons
  • Is the game free to play?
    Yes! The game is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases for special cards and upgrades.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes, the single-player campaign can be played offline, but multiplayer mode requires an internet connection.
  • How do I unlock new cards?
    You can earn cards through gameplay, daily rewards, special events, or by purchasing card packs.
  • Are there regular updates?
    Absolutely! We frequently add new cards, heroes, and game modes to keep the experience fresh and exciting.
